What is a forum?

A forum is a place to exchange ideas, or views on a particular topic or issue.

What exactly is this about?

Hi and welcome to my website! This forum focuses on Christianity and is available for Christians and non-Christians alike to learn more about God and talk about their experiences with Jesus. It doesn't matter who you are, everyone is welcome, whether you are just curious or sharing your story. If you ever feel too shy to share anything, you are obviously not obliged to share it, but I would like to remind you that this is kept completely anonymous. Thank you for using my site! God bless you all.

About the creators

Hi! We are two High School seniors that decided to test our coding skills out to create a personal project together. We knew there was no better way to start than to make a website honoring Christianity and God. This website is a personal project and something that we hope can be useful to young Christians(or older) as well as anyone interested in Christianity, or anyone that may have any questions about it. Thank you for using our website and have a wonderful day! God bless you all.
